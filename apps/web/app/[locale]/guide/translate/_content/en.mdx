## FAQ

**Which languages are supported?**
All major languages supported by the underlying model. Google and Microsoft cover
100+ languages. GPT/Claude/Gemini support fewer but with higher quality for common
languages.

**Why are some models greyed out?**
Pro-only models require an active Pro subscription. Click the model card to see
the upgrade prompt.

**Is language detection automatic?**
Yes. Set the source language to "Auto" and the engine will detect it for you.
Note: detection accuracy varies by model and text length.

**Are translations stored?**
Logged-in users have access to translation history. Anonymous sessions are not stored.
